# Legal pages (Impressum, Datenschutzerklärung)

German law (§5 TMG and the DSGVO) requires every commercial or public-facing German website to carry an Impressum and a Datenschutzerklärung. The club is a Verein, so both are non-optional. The site treats them as static, low-traffic pages reachable only from the footer.

## Finding the pages

Both pages are linked exclusively from the Footer — one row at the bottom of every page lists `Startseite | Instagram | Impressum | Datenschutzerklärung`. There is no header nav entry. The expectation is that regulators and the occasional curious visitor click through, not that ordinary users see these pages during normal browsing.

## Impressum

A single-column page that displays the responsible person's name and postal address, a phone number and email for contact, and the editorially responsible person.

The published email is a deliberately disposable alias rather than a primary inbox. A future change of alias should not require touching the visitor scenario.

### Example: the compliance checker

A regional trade-office inspector confirms the club site carries a complete Impressum and notes the name, street address, phone, and email of the responsible person.

### Example: the curious user with a complaint

A visitor spots a typo on a news article, reaches the Impressum via the footer, and emails the contact address.

## Datenschutzerklärung

A long-form privacy policy page covering, at minimum: an "at-a-glance" overview, identification of the data controller, what data is collected and how, visitor rights (access, correction, deletion, portability, objection), cookies and server logs, hosting and transport encryption, and the third-party services the site embeds — currently the Instagram feed.

The page is entirely static — no form, no consent banner, no analytics opt-out.

## What the legal pages do not do

- No cookie consent dialog. The site doesn't set analytics cookies, so it relies on the "technische Notwendigkeit" exemption.
- No GDPR data-request form — requests go through the Impressum email.
- No multilingual version; both pages are German-only.
- No "last updated" timestamp shown to the visitor.
